Scrambled Eggs>

Behold! It's the oldest dish of all time! Eggs. Yep, just eggs. Ok it may not be older than a chicken but.. I mean, what's not to like?

  • 3 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Whisk until homogenous mixture, adding salt and pepper to taste
  3. Heat pan up on medium until butter begins to melt
  4. Pour eggs in, reduce heat to low
  5. Stir around until cooked, make sure there are no brown spots.
