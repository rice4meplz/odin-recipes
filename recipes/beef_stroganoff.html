Beef Stroganoff

Before you lies the undisputed best dish that can be found in the eastern hemisphere. It is none other than Beef Stroganoff, not to be confused with pasta! Bleh.

To make such a delectable dish, you first must have the following ingredients:

  • Beef (shocker, I know)
  • Garlic Herbs and Cheese
  • Sugar Cream
  • Butter
  • Beef Stock
  • Pasta
  • Corn Starch
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Onion Dip Soup Mix
  1. Boil pasta to desired softness
  2. Cube preferred cut of beef and season with salt and pepper
  3. Set Instant Pot to sautee; add 3 tbsp olive oil and 2 tbsp butter
  4. Slightly brown the beef (DO NOT COOK)
  5. Take out the beef and place aside
  6. Sautee diced onions for one minute
  7. Add mushrooms, continue sautee
  8. Add freshly cut garlic
  9. Add 1tbsp of Dijon Mustard
  10. Add 1tbsp of white wine
  11. Add 1 1/2 cup of Beef Stock
  12. Pour in 1 bag of Onion Dip Soup Mix
  13. Place beef back into mixture and prepare to pressure cook
  14. Pressure cook on HIGH for 7 minutes
  15. Quick-release after 7 minutes, add 2 tsp of water/corn starch mix
  16. Add 8oz of Sour Cream
  17. Add 6oz of Garlic Herbs and Cheese

And once the soup is done, just throw the pasta you cooked earlier and bam, you've got an amazingly flavorful dish of Beef Stoganoff!
