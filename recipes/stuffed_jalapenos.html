Stuffed Jalapenos

It's stuffed jalapenos. Do you like jalapenos? Do you like cream cheese? Well here you go!

  • Jalapenos (obviously)
  • Cream Cheese
  • Bacon
  1. Into a bowl, mix cream chese with whatever seasonings
  2. Hallow out jalapenos
  3. Stuff jalapenos with cream cheese mixture
  4. Wrap bacon around jalapenos
  5. Cook in oven at 325 degrees Fahrenheit for 10 minutes
